Wenfei Xu is an urban data scientist, planner, and designer specializing in the intersection of spatial data analysis and visualization. She is currently a researcher at the Civic Data Design Lab in the Department of Urban Planning at MIT. She is directing a project on discovering residential vacancy across China through social media data, and a project on developing an open-source place-making / place-sensing urban furniture.

Her research interests include: causes and consequences of urbanization and analysis of gentrification trends, the role of amenities in urban vitality, civic engagement through crowd-sourced data collection methods, and spatial mapping and visualization.
